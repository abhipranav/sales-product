# ICP And Segments

## Beachhead (first 12 months)

- Founder-led sales teams
- Early startups with 2-20 sellers
- SMB/mid-market teams with weak RevOps support

## Expansion segments

- Mid-market organizations with established RevOps
- Enterprise teams only after compliance and audit maturity

## Why this order

- High urgency and low process complexity in startups
- Shorter feedback loops for workflow product iteration
- Faster path to proving time saved and follow-up reliability
